ABOUT

I'm a student at the University of Michigan majoring in Computational Cognitive Science. I was drawn to this program because I'm interested in the psychology behind coding, and how the way people interact with UI shapes their decisionmaking. I'm particularly interested in responsive web and graphic design, and I have experience working both independently, completing coursework and developing for clients.

Throughout my time at school, I've also been able to spend my time working at the core of the organizations I've had the opportunity to be a part of. I was able to coordinate performances and create original marketing material as the President of Marketing and Events for Blue Bop Jazz Orchesta, and plan volunteering opportunities and other productive events for Michigan sudents as the Activism Chair for LGBT+ Michigan.

In my free time, I really enjoy playing jazz drums and performing in another music organization on campus. I've also recently been picking up learning new recipies, but you'll have to ask my roomate whether that's a good investment of my time or not. I enjoy hiking, listening to new music and learning more about graphic design and front-end development.
